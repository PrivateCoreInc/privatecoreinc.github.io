Useful Links

This page contains links to a variety of useful data and cloud security resources.

  • Intel® Software Guard Extensions (Intel® SGX) Instructions and Programming Model

    This Intel presentation and paper describe the threat to data-in-use and how Intel SGX technology, when available and integrated into software, will reduce the attack surface and help move the security boundary to the CPU.

    Tags: Protected Memory Enclave

  • NIST Final Public Draft Special Publication 800-53 Revision 4, Security and Privacy Controls for Federal Information Systems and Organizations

    The US National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) document describing security and privacy controls for federal information systems and organizations.

    Tags: NIST

  • Hey, You, Get Off of My Cloud: Exploring Information Leakage in Third-Party Compute Clouds

    A paper by university researchers exploring the possibility of side channel attacks occurring in infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) environments.

    Tags: IaaS, Side Channel Attack

  • Physical Attack Protection with Human-Secure Virtualization in Data Centers

    An IEEE article highlighting the vulnerability of public cloud infrastructure to physical and insider attacks.

    Tags: IEEE

  • NIST SP 800-145, The NIST Definition of Cloud Computing

    The US National Institute of Standards and Technology document providing a definition of cloud computing.

    Tags: Cloud, NIST

  • NIST Computer Security Resource Center Supply Chain Risk Management

    The US National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Computer Security Division Resource Center webpage providing a background materials on information and communications technology Supply Chain Risk Management (SCRM).

    Tags: SCRM, NIST

  • Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) Cloud Controls Matrix (CCM)

    The CSA CCM provides fundamental security principles to guide cloud vendors and to assist prospective cloud customers in assessing the overall security risk of a given cloud services provider.

    Tags: Encryption, Cloud

  • OpenStack Trusted Computing Pools

    OpenStack provides open source software for building private and public clouds, and Trusted Computing Pools provides a method of created trustworthy compute node pools within OpenStack.

    Tags: OpenStack
